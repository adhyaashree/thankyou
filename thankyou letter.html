✔️ Do’s

  • Store in an airtight pouch or box after use.
  • Wipe gently with a soft dry cloth.
  • Keep away from moisture and store in a cool place.
  • Clean occasionally with mild soap and warm water.

❌ Don’ts

  • Avoid perfume, deodorant, and chemicals directly on jewellery.
  • Do not wear while bathing, swimming, or exercising.
  • Do not clean with toothpaste or abrasive materials.
  • Do not store different pieces together to prevent scratches.
